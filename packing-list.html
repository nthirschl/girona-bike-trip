Packing List

Multi-day gravel bike tour · Early March
Girona in early March Expect 8–16 °C (46–61 °F), partly cloudy with a chance of rain. Mornings can be cool. Pack layers.

On the Bike

  • 2x biking bottoms e.g. 1x bib + 1x normal shorts
  • 2x bike tops
  • Wind shell
  • Rain shell
  • Lightweight packable puffy/insulation
  • Lightweight gloves
  • Sunglasses
  • Buff/neck gaiter cold starts, wind protection
  • Cycling shoes optional — flat pedals + street shoes is fine

Off the Bike

  • 1–2x shirt
  • 1x pants
  • Off-bike shoes
  • 3x underwear
  • 3x socks

Bike Gear & Repair

  • Lightweight bike lock
  • Pedals unless borrowing
  • Helmet unless borrowing
  • Saddle optional — cumbersome but may be worth it
  • Multitool
  • Tire levers
  • Patch kit(s)
  • Spare tube TPU is nicest for packability
  • Mini pump or CO2 inflator
  • Quick-link for chain
  • Front bike light strong enough to illuminate road at night
  • Rear bike light red
  • Bike bags your personal bag setup
  • Chain lube will probably buy a small bottle in Girona

Tech & Navigation

  • Head unit at least 1 in the group
  • Phone
  • Roaming or eSIM plan for phone data in Spain
  • Backup battery Noah & Wynne bringing a couple
  • Chargers & cables for phone, head unit, lights
  • US → European plug adaptor Type C / Europlug

Essentials

  • Sunscreen
  • Toothbrush / toothpaste / floss
  • Other personal hygiene
  • Chamois butter
  • Small first aid kit Noah & Wynne packing; others welcome to also
  • Passport / ID
  • Cash + cards some small villages are cash-preferred
  • Water bottles at least 2 per person
  • Ear plugs shared accommodations
